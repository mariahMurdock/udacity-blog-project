Who What Where

A Style Blog

What When Where is a personal style blog based in Portland, Oregon focused on affordable, attainable style for the everyday woman.

Hi, I’m Mariah! I’m 25 years old, and I live in Portland, OR. with my partner, Alex. I started this blog in September 22nd of 2020 as a fun project to submit for my Udacity Program. I’ve always been a sucker for a great deal, and a sale shopper as long as I can remember! So naturally, I love finding & sharing great deals with everyone. As well as staple, worth-every-penny pieces! I’m drawn to everyday, cute, comfortable items. I hope that this creative project for me can be a source of inspiration and a fun pastime for my readers!
